4. What is holding you back

Review volume

You have 21 Google reviews. The dealers above you have 145, 968, and 996. Google treats review count as a measure of prominence, separate from your star rating. This is the single biggest factor in your position, and it only moves one way: a consistent system that invites every buyer to leave a review.

Your website is nearly invisible to Google

  • Your page title says only "Cochran Motor Company." It never says Rome, GA, used cars, or dealership, so Google has no signal about what you sell or where.
  • Your homepage and your inventory page carry identical titles and descriptions.
  • The site has no headline structure (no H1 headings) and no structured data, the machine-readable labels that tell Google "this is a dealership, this is a vehicle, this is the price."
  • When automated systems read your site, they see about 27 words of actual text. Your inventory loads through scripts those systems do not run.
