What this means

Your strongest markets are not the ones closest to Rome

Summerville, 18.6 miles out, is your best market anywhere: top three on 13 of 20 searches, first on 9, and you appear in 16 of 20. Calhoun, 21 miles out, is 12 and 6. Both beat Rome, where you hold the top three on 7 of 20 and first on 3.

This is not distance behaving strangely. Both towns contain one of your own offices, each with its own Business Profile, and in both towns those local listings are what hold the number one spots. In Summerville the listing holding first place on dental clinic, teeth cleaning and dental veneers is your Summerville office. In Calhoun it is your Calhoun office. A local listing in the town being searched is doing the heavy lifting.

Shannon deserves a mention too: 9 top-three placements and 4 first-place finishes in a community of about 1,900 people, with no office there at all.

Your five listings carry very different review bases

All five of your offices appear in these results, and Google treats each as a separate business with its own reputation:

  • Rome, 4.9 stars on 550 reviews
  • Calhoun, 5.0 on 196
  • Summerville, 4.9 on 144
  • Armuchee, 5.0 on 97
  • Cartersville, 5.0 on 20

Line that up against the market results and the correlation is hard to miss. The two listings with the deepest review bases, after Rome, are the two towns you dominate.

Cartersville has the office, the listing and the room

Cartersville returns 1 top-three placement out of 20, but that number understates the position badly. You appear in 11 of 20 searches there, and five of them are sitting at fourth, fifth or sixth: family dentist at 4th, general dentistry at 4th, sedation dentistry at 4th, cosmetic dentist at 5th and dental clinic at 6th. Three of those five are one single position outside the block patients actually see.

The businesses ahead of you in those five searches are Today's Dentistry at 561 reviews, Allatoona Family Dental at 387, Mockingbird Dental Associates at 289, DownEast Smile Co. at 237, Family Dentistry of Bartow County at 233 and Cartersville Dentist Office at 186. Your Cartersville listing sits at 20. It is a newer office and that number reflects how long it has been open rather than anything about the care being given, but it is the one variable that separates this office from the two that are winning their towns outright.

Worth noting that Cartersville is already working on one term: you hold third for smile makeover there, and interestingly both your Rome listing at third and your Cartersville listing at fourth appear in that same result.

The facial aesthetics searches belong to a different industry

This was flagged as a likely mismatch before measurement, and the data confirms it clearly. Across the five aesthetics terms, 80 searches in total, you reach the top three exactly once, in Calhoun for facial aesthetics. Medspa, dermal fillers and lip fillers return no appearance for you in any of the 16 markets, and botox returns three appearances and no top-three placement.

The reason is visible in who does hold those spots. The most frequent holders are Ultimate Beauty Medspa, Verity Aesthetics + Wellness Studio, Smiley Aesthetics Medical Spa, Reflections Medical Spa, WeCare Health and Wellness, beauty Bar Med Spa at Southern Surgical Arts, Georgia Aesthetics and The Beauty NP. Every one is a dedicated medspa or aesthetics practice. Not one dental practice appears anywhere in these packs, in any market.

Your profile is correctly categorised as a Dentist, and that is the right call for the other 240 searches in this report. These particular packs are simply built from a different category, and the outcome would be the same for any dental practice offering the same service. This is not a gap in your visibility and it is not worth spending effort against. The service is real; the search route to it is not this one.

Where you are missing, distance is not the explanation

Before concluding that a town is too far, it is worth checking whether anyone further away is winning it. In 50 of these searches, spread across 11 different markets, a business ranks in a town while being further from it than you are.

The clearest cases are close to home. In Lindale, 4.6 miles from your Rome office, there are 11 such searches, including Dental Town Emergency Services holding second for emergency dentist from 41.1 miles away. In Cave Spring, 14 miles out, there are 9, including Atlanta Sedation Dentistry holding first for sedation dentistry from 58.6 miles away. Cedartown has 7, Rockmart, Adairsville and Aragon have 4 each.

Those spots are not being decided by who is nearest. They are being decided by which businesses have told Google they cover the area.

Reviews explain part of the picture, not most of it

Across every search where a competitor ranks above you, 271 of those competitors have 550 reviews or fewer and 153 have more. So for the majority of the businesses outranking you, review volume is not what put them there.

Cartersville is the genuine exception, where every practice ahead of you on the five near-miss searches has between 122 and 561 reviews against that listing's 20. That is a real and specific case, and it is why it appears in the recommendations. Everywhere else, the lever is something other than reputation, because your reputation is already competitive.

Your broadest term is your weakest

The plain search dentist puts you in the top three in only 2 of 16 markets, Summerville and Calhoun, and in Rome you sit at sixth behind Rome Smiles at 1,200 reviews, 3 Rivers Cosmetic & Restorative Dentistry at 968, Edwards Family Dentistry at 697, Rome Dental Partners at 632 and Rome Dental at 420.

Compare that to smile makeover, which reaches the top three in 9 towns, or teeth cleaning and sedation dentistry at 7 each, or family dentist at 6 with 5 first-place finishes. The narrower, more specific searches carry you much further than the category word does.

Three things that would move the needle

Grow the Cartersville listing's review base

This is the highest-value move in the report and it costs nothing but a habit at the front desk. Cartersville has five searches sitting at fourth, fifth or sixth: family dentist, general dentistry, sedation dentistry, cosmetic dentist and dental clinic. Three of those five are at fourth, one position outside the visible block.

Every practice ahead of you in those searches carries between 122 and 561 reviews. That listing currently shows 20 at a 5.0 rating, which is what a newer office looks like rather than a reflection of the work. Your own results show what changes when that number grows: Calhoun at 196 reviews leads its town on 12 searches, Summerville at 144 leads on 13. Moving Cartersville toward that range would take the office from 1 top-three placement to a realistic 6, and everything else that market needs is already built. The building, the team and the listing exist and Google is already showing you to those searchers.

Lead with smile makeover and sedation dentistry

Your specific service terms travel much further than the category word. Smile makeover reaches the top three in 9 towns including Cartersville and Cedartown, two markets where almost nothing else of yours lands. Sedation dentistry reaches 7 towns with 4 first-place finishes. Teeth cleaning reaches 7. Family dentist reaches 6 and takes first in 5 of them. The plain word dentist reaches 2.

Most practices put the category first and file the specific services on an inner page. Your numbers say the reverse would serve you better. Making these named services the front of how each office presents itself puts your visibility behind the searches that are already carrying you, and it works across every one of your five locations rather than just one town.

Cover the towns sitting between your Rome and Cartersville offices

Euharlee, Rockmart and Cedartown sit in the corridor between two of your offices and return no top-three placements between them, yet the data says this is not a distance problem. Euharlee is 15.1 miles from Rome and already has you at fourth for family dentist and fifth for both smile makeover and sedation dentistry, while Atlanta Sedation Dentistry holds a spot there from 37.3 miles away. Rockmart shows the same shape, with Atlanta Sedation Dentistry ranking second from 41.4 miles. Cedartown has 7 searches held by businesses further away than you are.

These are contests being decided by declared coverage rather than proximity, which means they are open to you in a way that a town with an established local practice on the same street is not. Your Business Profiles are where that coverage gets stated, and with five offices across the region you have a stronger claim to this corridor than almost anyone currently ranking in it.
