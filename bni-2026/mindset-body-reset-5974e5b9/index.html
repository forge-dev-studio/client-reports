What this means

What you already own

Rome is close to a clean sweep of everything you are currently eligible for. You appear in 13 of its 20 searches and 11 of those land in the top three, seven of them at first: psychiatrist, psychiatric nurse practitioner, anxiety treatment, depression treatment, ADHD treatment, PTSD treatment and semaglutide weight loss. On that last one you rank ahead of a clinic carrying 4,200 reviews.

Lindale is nearly as good. You appear in 13 searches there, hold the top three in 9 and take first in 4. Those are unusually strong home-market numbers for a single-location practice, and they are the reason the rest of this report is about widening what you already do well rather than fixing something broken.

The primary care half of the practice returns nothing anywhere

All five primary care terms come back blank in all 16 towns: primary care doctor, family doctor, family medicine clinic, primary care clinic and walk-in clinic. That is 80 searches with no appearance at any position, not even twentieth, in a grid where you take first place eleven times elsewhere.

What makes this look like a filing question rather than a competitive one is how total it is. Google rendered a full local pack for every one of those searches, so there was always a list to be on. In the Rome primary care clinic pack, a business named Rome Primary Care LLC ranks fourth on a single review. In Cave Spring the family doctor search returns only three businesses in total, and the leader has 28 reviews. In Shannon, Primary Healthcare Centers holds third place from 18 miles out while your office is 7.8 miles from that town. Practices far smaller than yours are appearing in these packs and you are not appearing in any of them, which is the pattern you would expect when eligibility is being decided before ranking even starts.

Being straight about the ceiling: the Rome primary care packs are led by Harbin Clinic Family Medicine Rome at 4.9 on 979 reviews and Atrium Health Floyd Primary Care Family Medicine at 4.8 on 269. First place on "family doctor" in Rome is a long campaign. Appearing on the list at all is a much shorter question, and right now the answer to it is no everywhere.

One caveat on these numbers. Several of the Lindale primary care packs return clinics from a Lindale in another state, including UT Health East Texas Physicians and PhyNet Lindale Medical Clinic, so those particular cells are not a clean read of your local market.

Reviews are not the lever here

You are at 4.9 on 148 reviews. Of the businesses holding a top-three spot somewhere in this grid, 321 publish a review count, and 243 of them have fewer reviews than you do.

The weight loss packs make the point sharply. In Rome you are fourth for medical weight loss. Above you sit First Doctors Weight Loss at 4.8 on 215 reviews, then Lotus Health & Wellness Oasis at 5.0 on 16 and Premier Weight Loss & Medispa at 5.0 on 13. You are fifth for weight loss clinic, where three of the four above you carry 13, 31 and 16 reviews against your 148. Whatever is ordering those results, review count is not doing the work, and on that measure you are already ahead of nearly every business you are competing with.

Two term families belong to different kinds of business

"Walk-in clinic" was included as a deliberate mismatch check and it behaved exactly as expected. The Rome pack is urgent care chains: Wellstar Urgent Care at 4.8 on 493 reviews, Harbin Clinic Immediate Care Rome at 4.6 on 2,300, Piedmont Urgent Care at 4.8 on 1,500. A scheduled-appointment practice is not what that search is looking for, and it should not be read as a gap.

"Substance use treatment" turns out to work the same way. You appear in none of the 16 towns, and those packs are residential and detox facilities: Southeast Detox Center at 5.0 on 312, Fairland Recovery Center at 4.7 on 82, New Horizons Treatment Center at 4.0 on 38. In Rome the search returns only three businesses in total. That is a different vertical, not a missed opportunity.

A related measurement note: "weight loss doctor" in Rome returns exactly one business, First Doctors Weight Loss. A pack with a single entry is not a real contest in either direction.

Past about eight miles, the leaders are a long way off

There are 55 searches in this grid where a practice farther from that town than you are holds a pack spot, and 22 of those are first place. Three examples:

  • Shannon, 7.8 miles from your office, where you appear in 11 of 20 searches: first place for anxiety treatment, PTSD treatment and ADHD treatment all go to The Sylvia Brafman Mental Health Center from 53 miles away.
  • Cedartown, where you appear in 8 searches and reach the top three in none: Resilience Behavioral Health takes first for anxiety and depression treatment from 57 miles, and Clarity Integrative Psychiatry takes first for psychiatrist from 43.
  • Aragon, where Resolve Medical Weight Loss holds first for weight loss doctor, weight loss program and weight loss clinic from 38.4 miles while your office sits 16.6 miles away.

This matters because thin results in the outer towns are usually put down to distance, and here the data rules distance out. Those spots are being decided by what the profiles declare rather than by who is nearest to the patient.

Where the near misses sit

Fifteen searches across the region place you at fourth, fifth or sixth. Eight of the fifteen are weight loss terms, and six of those eight are in Rome, Lindale and Shannon, all inside eight miles of the office. That is the tightest cluster of nearly-there results on the map, and it is in the towns where you are already strongest.

Three things that would move the needle

Add the primary care side of the practice to your Business Profile

Eighty searches across sixteen towns return nothing for you, and every one of them is a primary care term. In the same grid you take first place eleven times on psychiatric and weight loss terms. Your profile carries one category, Mental health clinic, and the split in the results tracks that category almost exactly.

A Google Business Profile can carry a primary category plus additional ones, and those additional categories are a large part of how Google decides which packs a business is eligible to appear in. Someone searching for family medicine in Rome will not find an integrative primary care practice that Google has filed only under mental health. First place on those terms belongs to Harbin Clinic and Atrium Health Floyd for now and that is a long fight, but appearing on the list at all is a different question, and at the moment there is no version of this map where the primary care half of your practice can be found. This is a profile edit you can make yourself, it costs nothing, and it has more blank space behind it than anything else in the report.

Claim Shannon and Cedartown, where the leaders are fifty miles out

In 55 searches here a practice farther from the town than you are holds a pack spot, and 22 of those are first place. Shannon is 7.8 miles from your office and you already appear in 11 of its 20 searches, yet first place for anxiety treatment, PTSD treatment and ADHD treatment all go to a facility 53 miles away. Cedartown shows you in 8 searches with no top-three placement at all, while first for anxiety, depression and psychiatrist goes to practices 43 to 57 miles out.

The natural explanation for results thinning past the county line is distance, and in your case the data rules it out. Those packs are being decided by which practices declare that they cover the area and which services they list, not by proximity. Shannon and Cedartown are the two towns where the gap between where you sit and where you rank is widest, which makes them the cheapest ground on the map to pick up.

Move the weight loss searches where you sit fourth and fifth at home

Fifteen searches across the region place you at fourth, fifth or sixth, and eight of them are weight loss terms. Six of those eight are within eight miles of the office: medical weight loss and weight loss clinic in Rome, medical weight loss, weight loss clinic and weight loss program in Lindale, and weight loss clinic in Shannon.

You are already first in Rome for semaglutide weight loss, ahead of a clinic with 4,200 reviews, so Google clearly registers the practice as a weight loss provider. The gap is on the plainer terms, where three of the four businesses sitting above you in the Rome weight loss clinic pack carry 13, 16 and 31 reviews against your 148. Closing a one or two position gap on searches where Google already shows you, in the three towns where you rank best, is the shortest distance in this report between the current map and more calls to the front desk.
