What this means

What this market actually looks like

Every one of the 300 searches returned a full local pack, 20 businesses deep, with no exceptions on any keyword in any town. Not a single search anywhere on the grid came back thin. 203 different companies appeared in the top five. In a smaller market the specific terms usually thin out to a handful of results, which is where the openings are. Nothing thins out here.

Almost all of that competition is genuinely local. Of the 1,490 competitor placements captured, only 23 are held by businesses flagged as outside the search area. Metro Atlanta roofing packs are filled by metro Atlanta roofers, which means every position is contested by someone with a real claim to it.

It is worth saying plainly, because it changes what a good result looks like: the businesses at the top of these packs are large. Dr Roof Atlanta holds 81 positions and 25 first places on 1,900 reviews. Fraser Roofing carries 2,500 reviews, Findlay Roofing 3,000, Peachtree Roofing & Exteriors 838, DOM Roofing 734, Castle Roofing 642, Georgia Roof Advisors 501. Modest placement in this market is a different achievement from leading a small one.

Vinings is yours, and the last step is short

You appear in 16 of the 20 Vinings searches and hold the top three in nine. First place for "roof financing", second for "flat roof repair", and third for seven more: roofing company, roof replacement, roof repair, roof installation, roof inspection, emergency roof repair and roof insurance claim contractor.

Sitting at third on seven searches puts you inside the visible block on all of them. The single most valuable cell in this report is the one just outside it: "roofing contractor", the broadest phrase in the entire set, where you sit fourth in your own home market.

Of the 29 businesses that outrank you somewhere in Vinings, 18 carry fewer reviews than your 123. Two of them reach in from a long way out. StormROOF Systems ranks above you in Vinings from 66.6 miles away, and RT Gutters & Home Solutions from 110.2. Those placements are not being won on proximity.

Smyrna shows you the pattern

Smyrna is 3.6 miles from your office. You appear in 11 of its 20 searches, so Google plainly associates you with the town, but none of those 11 reaches the top three. Your best there is seventh, for "roof inspection".

Broad presence with no top placements is the signature of a business Google knows sits nearby but does not yet treat as a Smyrna business. The same shape repeats outward: Brookhaven four appearances with none higher than twelfth, Mableton three, Sandy Springs two, Atlanta two.

Where distance is the reason, and where it is not

In 114 of the 300 searches, at least one business holds that town from farther away than you are from it, and in 36 of those the farther business takes first place. The longest reach recorded is 72.8 miles.

Those cases cluster in specific towns, and that clustering is the useful part:

  • Atlanta: 17 such cases, six of them at first place. You appear twice.
  • Austell: 16 cases, four at first place, one from as far as 56.3 miles. You appear once.
  • Brookhaven: 14 cases, eight of them at first place. You appear four times.
  • Clarkston: 13 cases. You do not appear at all.

In those four towns, whoever is ranking is not doing it by being nearest. Something other than geography is deciding them, which makes them a contest you can enter.

The reverse is equally worth knowing before any effort goes anywhere. Roswell produced no such cases at all, Marietta one, Powder Springs one. Every business winning those packs is closer to the town than you are. Marietta is only 7 miles from you and Roswell 12.6, but they are locally saturated in a way Austell and Clarkston are not, and they are the hardest ground on this map.

Reviews are a real currency here, but not the one holding you back

You are at 5.0 stars on 123 reviews, an unusually clean record. Of the 203 competitors captured, 117 have fewer reviews than you and 118 carry a lower rating. Only 28 hold both a 5.0 rating and a larger count. Of the 178 competitor placements sitting above you in searches where you both appear, 73 come from businesses with fewer reviews than yours.

So reviews do not explain most of the gap. The nuance particular to this metro is that the very top of the broadest packs does skew toward companies carrying several hundred to several thousand. The fair reading is that your reputation is competitive, that volume matters in the largest and broadest searches, and that it is not what decides the specific ones.

The specialists own the sub-categories, and they do it on very few reviews

This is the most surprising thing in the data. On the commercial, gutter and siding terms, the packs belong to businesses built around that one service, and several of them are winning on review counts a fraction of yours.

  • Absolute Commercial Roofing holds 40 positions and 29 first places on 121 reviews.
  • Atlanta Commercial Roofing Contractors holds 27 positions and six first places on 11 reviews.
  • GA Industrial Roofing takes three first places on 8 reviews, and Atlanta Commercial Roofing Company holds 8 positions on 2.
  • Urban Seamless Gutters ATL takes first place on 10 of the 15 "gutter installation" searches, and Joe's Gutters & Covers four firsts on gutter guards.
  • Siding is held by Siding of Atlanta on 59 reviews, Ideal Siding Atlanta on 41 and Siding Unlimited on 30.

Against that, you do not appear anywhere for "gutter installation", "gutter guards installation", "commercial roofing company" or "industrial roofing contractor", and you appear once each for gutter replacement, siding installation, commercial roofing contractor and commercial roof replacement. Exteriors is half your name and the commercial terms were measured because they are part of the work, so this is the largest untouched area in the report. The review counts above say the barrier to entering it is low.

Two terms already travel further than the rest

"Roof insurance claim contractor" reaches eight of the 15 markets and "emergency roof repair" reaches seven. Nothing else in the set reaches more than four. Those are your storm and claims terms, and they are already doing the work of carrying you outside Vinings, into Sandy Springs, Mableton, Smyrna, Atlanta, Austell, Brookhaven, Dunwoody, Chamblee and Doraville.

One note on "roof financing", which holds your only first place. It was flagged in advance as a term that can pull lenders and finance companies instead of contractors. In this grid it did not. Every business holding those packs is a roofing company, so that first place is a real one.

Three things that would move the needle

Close the one-place gap on your biggest term in Vinings

"Roofing contractor" is the broadest phrase in the whole keyword set, the one a homeowner reaches for before they know what they need, and in Vinings you sit fourth on it. That is one position outside the block customers actually look at, in the single market where you already hold nine top-three placements and a first place.

Seven of those nine sit at exactly third, which is the bottom edge of the visible group. Strengthening the Vinings signals specifically, so the profile and the site read as a Vinings business rather than a metro-wide one, moves the head term into view and gives those seven some cushion at the same time.

This is the shortest gap on the map and everything hard is already in place. Google is showing you to these searchers. The work is moving up one or two rows, not winning new ground.

Put Austell, Clarkston, Brookhaven and Atlanta on your coverage map

These four towns are where the data says geography is not the obstacle. Across them there are 60 instances of a business winning one of those packs from farther away than you are from the town, 19 of them at first place, including one Austell pack held from 56.3 miles out. You are between 6.9 and 13.3 miles from all four.

Your presence across those same four is four appearances in Brookhaven, two in Atlanta, one in Austell and none in Clarkston, out of 20 searches each. Making sure your Business Profile declares the metro towns you actually work in, and that the website carries something real for the ones that matter most, is the highest-reach change available and it does not depend on out-competing anyone on review volume.

This is also the item you can handle yourselves this week without hiring anyone.

Spell out the commercial and exteriors work

You do not appear in any of the 15 markets for "gutter installation", "gutter guards installation", "commercial roofing company" or "industrial roofing contractor", and you appear exactly once each for gutter replacement, siding installation, commercial roofing contractor and commercial roof replacement.

What makes this worth the effort rather than a lost cause is who currently holds that ground. Atlanta Commercial Roofing Contractors takes six first places on 11 reviews. GA Industrial Roofing takes three on 8. Siding Unlimited holds position on 30 reviews and Ideal Siding Atlanta on 41. Set against your 123 reviews at a 5.0 rating, none of these are beating you on reputation. They are the businesses whose listings and websites say unmistakably that this one thing is what they do.

Giving the commercial roofing, flat roof, gutter and siding work its own explicit space, rather than a line inside a general description, is the largest piece of open ground in this report.
