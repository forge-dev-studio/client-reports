What the numbers show

1. The home territory is locked down

Inside 20 miles, S&S is in the top 3 for 89% of every search measured. Cedartown and Rockmart are first for 19 of 20 services each. This is as strong as a local listing gets, and it is the base everything else is built on.

2. Tallapoosa and Bowdon are standout performers

Tallapoosa (19 miles, population 3,212) is in the top 3 for 18 of 20 searches — the third-strongest market S&S holds anywhere. Bowdon (33 miles, population 2,155) matches it at 18 of 20, and does it from twice the distance.

Both are small towns punching well above their size, and both are currently earning that visibility without a dedicated page of their own on the website. Giving them one is the most direct way to turn existing rankings into phone calls.

3. The cost question is the single strongest asset S&S has

“How much does a land survey cost” is the question people ask before they ask anything else, and S&S owns it. Statewide, for “how much does a land survey cost in Georgia”, the S&S guide ranks #6 in the country's largest search engine against every surveyor in the state.

Town by town it is stronger still. Across 60 markets, S&S appears in the top 10 for 59 of them — 98% — at an average position of 3.6, with 36 of those inside the top 3. A single guide page does most of that work.

No other page, service or listing in this report reaches that many markets. It is the clearest proof that well-made content travels far past the range a map listing can reach.

4. Construction staking travels furthest of any service

It is in the top 3 in 20 markets and appears in 55 of 60. Nothing else comes close; the next best manages twelve. Of the nine top-3 finishes beyond 45 miles, five are construction staking — Grantville at 59 miles, Ball Ground at 56, LaFayette at 48, Palmetto at 47, South Fulton at 46.

That is a commercial signal worth leaning into. Builders and site contractors search by service and hire at distance, and the field competing for this term is thin. It is the most effective route S&S has into markets beyond the home ring.

5. Some searches favour the website over the listing

A few terms behave differently from the rest, and it is useful to know which.
