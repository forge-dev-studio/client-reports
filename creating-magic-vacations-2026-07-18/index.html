What this looks at

A snapshot of a moving picture, taken very early.

When someone searches something like "travel agent Cedartown GA", Google answers in two separate places. First a short list of nearby businesses with the little map, and then the ordinary web results underneath. They are ranked by different rules, so this report tracks both: your Google Business listing and your website.

Worth knowing before you read the numbers. Your website went live roughly two weeks ago, and the thirty-seven town pages on it are still being discovered and indexed by Google. That process normally takes several weeks to a couple of months. So the website column here is not a finished picture, it is the very first reading, and a good deal of what is already showing has appeared remarkably quickly.

Everything below was measured on one day in July. Search results shift a little day to day, so treat any single number as approximate and the overall shape as the real signal.
