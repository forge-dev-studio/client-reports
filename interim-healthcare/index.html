What this measures

The map pack, the three businesses Google shows with the map, takes the large majority of calls from local searches. Everything below it splits the rest.

For every one of 460 cells we ran the literal search a family would type, for example home health care cartersville ga, with the search centered on that town, and recorded where each Interim HealthCare listing appears and who holds the top three. This is the same signal paid tools like Local Falcon read. Positions drift a spot day to day; the pattern is what matters.

Keyword selection was evidence based, not guesswork: we crawled the five highest reviewed home care agencies in the north Atlanta metro (review bases of 101 to 690) to see which services they build pages for, then checked every candidate against Google's own search volume data for Georgia and for Rome, and kept the 20 highest value terms across three service families. "Near me" searches can't be measured per town from outside, so they are excluded here; note that home care near me is the single most searched home care term in Rome (70/mo), and it is won with the same profile signals this report recommends.

The proof it's fixable is inside your own brand

A sibling Interim franchise, same corporate website template, packs 10 of 20 searches in its market.

Interim HealthCare of Paulding County (5.0★, 26 reviews) appears 45 times in this grid from its Dallas/Hiram territory, including 10 pack positions, 10 of them in Acworth, a contested Cobb county border market. Same brand. Same corporate location page format. Roughly double the reviews, a 5.0 rating, and a locally tuned profile.

That matters because it removes the easy excuse. The corporate website template is not what's holding the Rome office to #5. The difference between the two offices is the strength of the local Google Business Profile: rating, review count and velocity, categories, and the services list. Those are exactly the levers a local SEO program controls.
